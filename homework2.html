Economy of Armenia

Daily news online




The Gross Domestic Product (GDP) in Armenia was worth 11.54 billion US dollars in 2017. The GDP value of Armenia represents 0.02 percent of the world economy. GDP in Armenia averaged 5.56 USD Billion from 1990 until 2017, reaching an all time high of 11.66 USD Billion in 2008 and a record low of 1.20 USD Billion in 1993.

In the latest reports, Nominal GDP of Armenia reached 2.3 USD bn in Mar 2018. Its GDP deflator (implicit price deflator) increased 4.3 % in Mar 2018. GDP Per Capita in Armenia reached 3,872.0 USD in Dec 2017. Its Gross Savings Rate was measured at 7.6 % in Mar 2018.
